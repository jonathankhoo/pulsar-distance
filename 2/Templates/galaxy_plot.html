As no spacecraft from Earth has ever travelled out of our Galaxy it is impossible for us to take a photo of our Galaxy from the outside. However, astronomers can see many other galaxies in the Universe and have evidence to show that our Galaxy is a spiral galaxy. Even though we cannot photograph our own Galaxy, we can make a diagram of its structure. Such a diagram is given below which represents looking down on our Galaxy from above. The yellow circle shows the position of our Solar System. [@PROCESSED_TEXT]
